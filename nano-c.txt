nano Commands
 *Save File: Ctrl + O
 *Exit nano: Ctrl + X
 *Cut Text: Ctrl + K
 *Paste Text: Ctrl + U
 *Search: Ctrl + W
 *Replace: Ctrl + \
 *Mark Text: Ctrl + ^ (Ctrl + Shift + 6)
 *Justify Text: Ctrl + J

**Text Formatting**
	(single star or underscore)*This is italic text* and _this is also italic_
	(double star or underscore)**This is bold text** and __this is also bold__

Insert ordered list:
	1. First item
	2. Second item
	3. Third item

Insert unordered list;
	* Item 1
	* Item 2
	* Item 3
